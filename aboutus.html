Our Story

At D'Bayong Art, we believe that every handcrafted piece carries a story, one woven through generations of Filipino tradition, artistry, and passion. What began as a vision to celebrate the beauty of indigenous weaving has grown into a proudly Filipino social enterprise dedicated to creating timeless artisan bags and lifestyle pieces. By blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, we transform natural materials into elegant creations that honor our cultural heritage while meeting the lifestyle of today's discerning customers.

Every D'Bayong Art piece is meticulously handcrafted by skilled Filipino artisans using locally sourced materials such as banig, pandan, hablon, abaca, and other indigenous fibers. Each design is individually conceptualized and carefully finished, ensuring that no two pieces are ever alike.

Our Philosophy Is Simple

One Bag. One Design.
Never Repeated.

Every piece is individually conceptualized and handcrafted, ensuring no two bags are ever alike — a true work of wearable Filipino art.

This commitment to exclusivity means every bag is a unique work of wearable art, crafted not only to be beautiful but to preserve the authenticity of Filipino craftsmanship. Beyond creating exceptional products, D'Bayong Art exists to create meaningful impact. Every purchase helps sustain local weaving traditions, empowers artisan communities, generates livelihood opportunities, and keeps centuries-old Filipino craftsmanship alive for future generations.

As we continue to grow, we embrace innovation while staying true to our roots. From handcrafted luxury collections to future digital authentication through NFC technology, we are building a brand where heritage, sustainability, craftsmanship, and technology come together.
