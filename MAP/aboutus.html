About Us

D'Bayong Art is a passionate endeavor dedicated to preserving and elevating the traditional Filipino craft of Bayong making. We believe in the beauty and functionality of handcrafted items, and we strive to bring this artistry to a wider audience. Each Bayong bag we create is a testament to the skill and creativity of our artisans, blending traditional techniques with contemporary designs.

Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the cultural heritage embedded in every weave. We are committed to sustainable practices, using locally sourced materials and empowering our community of artisans. With every Bayong you carry, you're not just carrying a bag, but a piece of Filipino artistry and a story of tradition and transformation.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote the art of Bayong making, empowering local artisans and celebrating Filipino craftsmanship through sustainable and innovative designs.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized brand that showcases the beauty and versatility of Bayong bags, while fostering cultural appreciation and sustainable livelihoods.
